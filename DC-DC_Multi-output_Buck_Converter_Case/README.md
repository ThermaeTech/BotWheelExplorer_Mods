# Protective Housing for DFRobot DFR1015 DC-DC Buck Converter

This is a 3D-printable protective case specifically designed for the DFRobot DC-DC Multi-output Buck Converter ([DFR1015](https://www.dfrobot.com/product-2599.html)).
The housing is designed to shield the circuit board from dust and physical damage, and serves as a compact enclosure to facilitate easy integration into your electronics projects.

## Key Features & Specifications

- Target Module: DFRobot DC-DC Multi-output Buck Converter (DFR1015)
- Purpose: Provides physical protection and ease of project integration.
- Mounting Method: Secure module fixation using two M2 threaded brass heat-set inserts.
- Required Hardware: M2 Inserts (x2) and M2 x 8 mm Screws (x2)

## Assembly Instructions

1. Install Inserts: Heat-set two M2 threaded inserts into the designated holes on the case bottom.
※Recommended Insert: M2 with an outer diameter of approximately φ3.2 mm.
2. Fix Module: Use the two M2 x 8 mm screws to secure the DC-DC module to the bottom part of the case.

## Recommended Print Settings

- Material: ABS or ASA
- Wall Layers: 3
- Top Layers: 3
- Bottom Layers: 3
- Infill: 10%
- Layer Height: 0.2 mm
